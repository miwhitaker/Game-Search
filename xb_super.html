Super Mega Baseball 3

Features:

  • Single player
  • 2 players split screen
  • 2 players online co-op
  • 2 players online versus

Systems:

  • PS4
  • XBox One
  • Switch

Other:

  • ESRB Rating: E (everyone)
  • Genre: Sports
  • Developer: Metalhead Software
  • Release Date: May 13, 2020
Run up the score in a relaxed slugfest or push the limits of your reflexes in this refined baseball simulator. Super Mega Baseball 3 features an all-new Franchise mode, major graphical enhancements, and on-field additions including pickoffs and situational player traits
Customize teams, players, logos, and uniforms. Challenge your hand-eye coordination or relax and hit dingers with fluid gameplay that scales seamlessly from novice to beyond- expert levels of difficulty.
