NBA 2k Playgrounds 2

Features:

  • Single player
  • Two player split screen
  • Two player online co-op
  • Online multiplayer

Systems:

  • PS4
  • XBox One
  • Switch

Other:

  • ESRB Rating: E (everyone)
  • Genre: Sports
  • Developer: Mad Dog Games
  • Release Date: October 18, 2018

BALL WITHOUT LIMITS

NBA arcade action is back with NBA 2K Playgrounds 2! The sequel to the original smash hit takes street balling to the next level with a massive roster of current and retired NBA players, improved online matchmaking with dedicated servers, four-player online matches, three-point contests, new playgrounds, custom matches, and more! Pick your team, get ready to jam, and Ball Without Limits!

EXCITING GAME MODES

Take street balling to the next level with a variety of exciting features, including new Season and Playgrounds Championship modes! Play 2-on-2 locally with friends, four-player online games with improved matchmaking, or head-to-head three-point contests. Build your own playgrounds in Court Editor mode with tons of customization options available!

EXPANDED ROSTER

Collect over 450 NBA players, from all-time greats Michael Jordan, Kobe Bryant & Dr. J, to current stars Karl-Anthony Towns, Jayson Tatum & Ben Simmons. Featuring new player models and signature player animations.

ALL-NEW PLAYGROUNDS

Flash your moves around the world on 17 all-new playground courts. Hit jumpers beachside in the California sun, prove your game in outer space in the Mars base or head down under and ball in the Australian outback.
