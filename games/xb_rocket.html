Rocket League

Features:

  • Single player
  • 2 players split screen
  • 2 players online co-op
  • 2 players online versus

Systems:

  • PS4
  • XBox One
  • Switch

Other:

  • ESRB Rating: E (everyone)
  • Genre: Sports
  • Developer: Psyonix
  • Release Date: July 7, 2015
Rocket League is a high-powered hybrid of arcade-style soccer and vehicular mayhem with easy- to-understand controls and fluid, physics-driven competition.
Cross-Platform Play Play with your friends online across all platforms! Kick-off in Soccar modes, or try Extra Modes that put a twist on classic Rocket League. Plus, enjoy the offline Season Mode!
Rocket Pass How far will you go in Rocket League's season pass? Play for free, or purchase Rocket Pass Premium and get unique In-Game Items, Credits, XP Bonus, and Weekly Challenges!
Competitive Seasons Play in online Competitive Playlists and climb the ranks! Each rank earns unique Competitive Rewards each season!
Item Shop & Blueprints Make your car your own with more than 500 trillion customization possibilities! Get in-game items for free after matches, browse the Item Shop, or build Blueprints for premium content for your car.
